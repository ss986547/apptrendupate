### 5. Ideas to SKIP This Week

1.  **General-Purpose AI Chatbot (e.g., a "better ChatGPT"):**
    *   **Why skip:** The market is dominated by tech giants (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Meta, Microsoft) with massive resources. Low-star reviews highlight fundamental issues (hallucinations, slowness, bans) that are incredibly difficult for a solo dev to solve at scale. Competing on raw model power or breadth of knowledge is a losing battle. The opportunity lies in *specialization and user experience*, not generalist replacement.
2.  **Developer Tools for "Agentic Engineering" or "Vibe Coding":**
    *   **Why skip:** While trending on HN and Product Hunt, these are highly technical, niche tools primarily for other developers. The solo indie dev's target market is typically broader consumers, and the complexity of building robust agentic systems or code generation tools in 1-3 months is extremely high. Monetization for developer tools often relies on B2B sales or complex licensing, which is outside the dev's typical model.
3.  **Ad-based "Cleaner" or "VPN" Apps:**
    *   **Why skip:** While "Cleaner Guru" and "VPN - Super Unlimited Proxy" rank high in Utilities, they often rely on aggressive ad monetization or questionable subscription practices. This directly contradicts the developer's preference for paid-up-front or subscription apps. These categories are also rife with low-quality apps and user distrust, making it hard to build a reputable, sustainable business.
