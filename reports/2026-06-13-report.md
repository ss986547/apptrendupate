5.  **Poor User Experience & Lack of Integration:** Users complain about confusing UI changes, lack of essential features like hands-free operation, or critical data loss (e.g., lost projects), and a lack of seamless cross-device integration.
    *   **Sources:** "Interface is trash... OpenAI keeps pushing updates that change the app interface and obscure features" (1⭐ ChatGPT), "Zero Integration...
