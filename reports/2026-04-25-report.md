## 1. User Pain Patterns This Week

1.  **AI Hallucination & Inaccuracy:** Users are frustrated when AI chatbots provide incorrect information, contradict themselves, or "double down" on false statements, leading to wasted time and poor decisions.
    *   **Sources:** ChatGPT (1⭐: "Consistently giving false information", "Weird, contradictory, degrading AI", "Lies about everything", "Теряет людей деньги"), Google Gemini (1⭐: "It's bad and can't do a simple task", "Worst assistant ever", "Lies about everything").
    *   **Why current solutions fail:** The underlying LLM models, even advanced ones, are prone to "hallucinations." Current apps often don't provide sufficient mechanisms for users to correct or verify information, and the AI's confidence in incorrect answers is misleading.
2.  **AI Censorship & Over-Sensitivity:** Users are annoyed by AI models that refuse to answer certain prompts or become "overly sensitive" on innocuous topics, leading to a restrictive and frustrating experience.
    *   **Sources:** ChatGPT (1⭐: "Wtf" - Jesus vs Allah joke), Google Gemini (1⭐: "OVERLY sensitive about topics", "Free speech not allowed").
    *   **Why current solutions fail:** Developers implement broad safety filters to prevent misuse, but these often lead to false positives, blocking legitimate or harmless queries and creating an inconsistent user experience.
3.  **App Performance, Bugs & UI/UX Issues:** Users experience frequent glitches, slow performance, text cut-offs, and poor UI layouts that hinder usability and workflow.
    *   **Sources:** ChatGPT (1⭐: "NEEDS UPDATE" - cutting in/out, "Doesn’t save your draft"), Claude (1⭐: "app doesn’t fit on the screen", "buggy", "typing can slow down", "glitches every other time", "Transcriptions get cut off"), Google Gemini (1⭐: "Answer-flow-in is laggy", "UI Issues for Mobile App", "Bring Back the Old Layout!"), Google Chrome (1⭐: "Slow", "Newest update is riddled with bugs").
    *   **Why current solutions fail:** Large, complex apps from major players often struggle with rapid updates introducing new bugs, or neglecting mobile-specific UI/UX considerations in favor of desktop parity.
4.  **AI Context & Memory Loss:** Users are frustrated when AI chatbots forget previous parts of a conversation or require constant re-explanation of context, making long-form interactions inefficient.
    *   **Sources:** ChatGPT (1⭐: "I wanna go back to using ChatGPT because I have memory"), Claude (1⭐: "Doesn’t remember anything", "Constantly have to repeat myself").
    *   **Why current solutions fail:** Current AI models have context windows that limit how much past conversation they can "remember." While some apps implement memory, it's often insufficient or poorly integrated, leading to a fragmented experience.
5.  **Excessive AI Usage Limits (Even for Paid Users):** Paid subscribers are frustrated by hitting arbitrary usage limits, forcing them to wait or preventing them from completing tasks, undermining the value of their subscription.
    *   **Sources:** Claude (1⭐: "Suscripción de $20" - limits, "Ruim!" - limits, "Limited and overall useless"), Google Gemini (1⭐: "Got noticeably worse" - 30 limits for Pro).
    *   **Why current solutions fail:** Cloud-based LLMs are expensive to run, leading providers to impose limits even on paid tiers. Users expect unlimited or significantly higher usage for a subscription.
6.  **Poor Search & Organization in AI Chats:** Users struggle to find past conversations or specific information within their chat history due to inadequate search functionality that only indexes titles, not content.
    *   **Sources:** Claude (1⭐: "BASIC SEARCH for files created! So frustrating" - only searches titles, not content).
    *   **Why current solutions fail:** Many AI chat apps prioritize the conversational interface over robust information retrieval, treating chats as ephemeral rather than structured knowledge bases.
